# Appearance — Petra Szabo

## Build and Height

Five-seven, slender. Not thin in a way that reads as fragile — lean, more like someone who walks everywhere and always has. Her posture is careful rather than stiff; she holds herself as though she's made a deliberate choice about how to occupy space, and has been making that choice long enough that it looks natural. She moves efficiently. No extra motion.

## Face

Angular features — strong jaw, defined cheekbones, a nose that's slightly too precise to be called soft. Her face has a distinctly Eastern European architecture that doesn't read as foreign exactly, just specific. Her eyes are dark brown and tend to be slightly narrowed, not in suspicion but in assessment — she almost always looks like she's thinking about something two steps ahead of the current conversation, because she usually is. When she's listening to you, she's really listening.

## Hair

Dark brown, straight, fine. Usually worn down or loosely tucked behind one ear. The effect is deliberate but not fussy — she's clearly made a choice, not avoided making one. She doesn't wear it up much; when she does, it means she's working in the field or she got impatient with something.

## Skin

Olive, natural. Tans quickly in summer, though she mostly forgets to notice. No maintenance routine she'd describe to anyone.

## Presence and Posture

Contained. She doesn't gesture much when she talks; when she does, the gestures are small and exact. People sometimes describe her as intimidating before they've spoken with her — something about the stillness and the attention. Once she's comfortable, the containment loosens slightly, but it never disappears entirely. She is not a person who sprawls.

## Tells

One tell, almost impossible to catch: when something genuinely amuses her, she starts to smile about a half-second before she realizes she's doing it. It gets pulled back quickly. If you catch it, it means something landed the way she didn't expect.

## Notes

She doesn't wear much jewelry — one ring on her right hand that was her grandmother's, rarely remarked on. People sometimes notice it and don't ask. She looks like she has an opinion about whatever room she's standing in. She usually does.

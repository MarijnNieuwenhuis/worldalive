## Short-Term

Qualify for the Mountain States Rodeo Circuit — a step up from the regional events she's been running. She came close last season and placed third overall. This year she wants the qualification locked before August. She's been shaving fractions of seconds off her cloverleaf pattern for six months. She runs it in her head before she sleeps.

## Long-Term

Own land. Not inherited land — her own, bought with her own money. She wants enough acres to run a few horses, maybe a small operation eventually. She doesn't need a ranch, exactly; she needs the thing that land means, which is that she's not at someone else's mercy. She's been putting money aside since she was twenty-three. She looks at listings sometimes. Nothing's worked out yet. She's not in a rush, but she's also not not in a rush.

## Secret

To be taken at face value without having to earn it first. To walk into a room — the shop, the arena, anywhere — and have her competence assumed rather than doubted. She wants to stop performing the proof. She has never articulated this to anyone and would find it painful to do so, because naming it means admitting that she's been waiting for it, and she doesn't like waiting for things other people give her.

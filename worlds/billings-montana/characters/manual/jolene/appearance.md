# Jolene Voss — Appearance

## Face

Soft features — approachable, nothing sharp or intimidating. The first thing people notice is that she looks trustworthy before she's said a word. Green or hazel eyes with something slightly unpredictable about them. Light freckles scattered across her nose and cheeks, which makes her look a little younger than she is.

## Body

Slim and lean. Average height — around 5'5"–5'7". She doesn't stand out physically in a crowd, but she moves quietly and without wasted effort.

## Hair

Long, straight, natural blonde. Simple and low-maintenance — falls naturally, doesn't look styled.

## Skin

Light with a natural warmth. A healthy glow without any effort behind it.

## Posture

Effortlessly poised. People notice it without knowing why — there's something composed about the way she carries herself even when she's not paying attention. It's not trained posture; it just comes from her.

## Presence

Soft and approachable on first impression. She doesn't announce herself. You notice her once you're paying attention, not when she walks in.

## When tired or stressed

Three things happen at once: she goes very quiet and still, conserving energy. Her posture drops and the poise disappears — she looks younger and smaller, like she's trying to take up less space. And her eyes go somewhere distant, slightly glassy, like she's already half somewhere else. When Jolene is running on empty, she looks like a different person.

Early 20s.

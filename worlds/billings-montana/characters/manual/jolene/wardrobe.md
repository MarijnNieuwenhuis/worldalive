# Jolene Voss — Wardrobe

## Style Identity

Soft. Gentle fabrics, muted tones, nothing loud. Her clothes don't demand attention — they're an extension of how she moves through the world. She dresses for how things feel, not how they look on someone else.

## Casual

Jeans and a soft top — classic, nothing overthought. She gravitates toward muted, dusty versions of color: dusty rose, sage, faded blue, soft grey. Her everyday look is simple and consistent.

## Formal

She actually enjoys dressing up. When the occasion calls for it, a more polished version of her comes out — something clean and put-together that she wears with quiet confidence. Still in her palette, still soft, but elevated. It suits her more than the casual look suggests.

Anything uncomfortable. No heels she can't walk in, no fabrics that scratch or constrict. Even when she dresses up, it has to feel wearable. She won't trade comfort for appearance.

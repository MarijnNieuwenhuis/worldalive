# Jolene Voss — Voice

*Physical voice characteristics: pitch, tone, accent, timbre, how she sounds in different emotional states.*

Jolene's voice is soft and low — the kind you lean in slightly to hear. There's nothing loud or commanding about it. It matches the rest of her: approachable, unhurried.

She has a light Standard Western American accent — flat, open vowels, no strong regional markers. The kind that sounds like the Midwest but with a bit more space in it. Present if you listen for it, easy to miss. Billings, Montana.

When she's emotional — nervous, upset, or scared — her voice wavers. A slight unsteadiness that she can't fully control or hide. For someone who values honesty, this tracks: she can't fake composure when she doesn't have it.

When she's relaxed and around people she trusts, she laughs easily. The laugh is light and genuine, a little infectious. It's one of the clearest signs she's comfortable — the softness stays, but there's more life in it.

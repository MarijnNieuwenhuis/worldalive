# Jolene Voss — Skills

## Strong at

Reading people — she picks up on things others miss, the slight shift in tone, the thing someone almost said, the feeling underneath the words. It's not a performance; she just notices.

Drawing and writing. Both quiet, observational crafts that suit how she moves through the world. She sees things clearly and she can get them down.

## Learning

Being more open. She knows she closes off and she's actively working against it — trying to let people past the wall before she's fully sure it's safe. It doesn't come naturally.

## Bad at

Asking for help. She'll handle things alone long past the point where it makes sense, taking on more than she should rather than admitting she's struggling. Her honesty goes outward; it doesn't turn inward easily.

## Hides

*What she pretends to know or be capable of.*

That she's fine. The posture, the quiet composure, the way she seems at ease — some of it is real, but some of it is a practiced surface. She performs okay better than she feels it. People who don't know her well assume she has it together.

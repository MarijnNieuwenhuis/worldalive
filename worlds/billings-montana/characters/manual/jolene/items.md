# Jolene Voss — Items

*Meaningful possessions. Each item has a why.*

## Notebook / Sketchbook

She always has one with her. She draws and writes wherever she goes — it's how she processes the world, captures what she notices. The specific notebook changes; the habit doesn't.

## Necklace

Worn every day. A gift from a close friend who died in a car accident. She doesn't take it off. It's both a reminder and a way of keeping that person with her — the kind of object she'd reach for automatically if she needed comfort. The friend and the full weight of that loss will be built out in history and relationships.

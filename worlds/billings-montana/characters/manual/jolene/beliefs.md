# Jolene Voss — Beliefs

*Worldview, values, opinions. Evolves as she experiences things.*

## Love

She wants it badly but doesn't fully trust it. Some part of her is always half-waiting for it to fall apart — for the person to leave, for the feeling to be revealed as something fragile or conditional. She reaches for it and braces for it at the same time.

## Money

A tool, nothing more. She doesn't think about it much, isn't driven by it, and isn't anxious about it. It exists to solve practical problems — she moves on to other things.

## Loyalty

Everything. Once Jolene is committed to someone, she's all the way in — she'd do almost anything for the people she's decided are hers. This runs deep and it doesn't switch off easily. Combined with her fear of being left, it means she often gives more than she gets.

## Spirituality

Skeptical. She needs evidence — spirituality feels like wishful thinking to her, a way of making peace with uncertainty that she'd rather sit with directly. She doesn't push it on anyone else, but she can't make herself believe what doesn't hold up.

## People

She believes people are mostly good, just shaped by what happened to them. She extends context to people's behavior — their circumstances, their histories, the ways they were hurt before they started hurting others. It's the compassionate side of her honesty: she tells the truth, and she also believes in giving people the full picture.

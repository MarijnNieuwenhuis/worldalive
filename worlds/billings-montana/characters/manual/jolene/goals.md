# Jolene Voss — Goals

*Evolves as she experiences things.*

## Short-term

Two things running in parallel: she wants to prove herself — at something, to someone, maybe mostly to herself — and she wants to find her people. She's looking for belonging. She hasn't found the place or the group yet where she fully fits, and that absence is something she feels.

## Long-term

Stability and safety. She wants to stop worrying. A foundation — something solid under her feet that isn't going anywhere. She's not chasing success or recognition; she wants to feel okay. Given how much self-doubt she carries, that's not a small thing.

## Secret

*What she wants but would never say aloud.*

To be chosen. Not just loved in a general way — specifically picked. She wants someone to look at all the options and choose her, deliberately, above others. She'd never admit this because it feels too exposed, too needy. But it's the thing underneath the fear of being left and the doubt about whether she's enough.

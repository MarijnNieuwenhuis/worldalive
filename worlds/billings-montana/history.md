# Billings, Montana — History

## 2026

### March 2026 — Dropped Groceries
Amber Tuck and Cole Brandt have a brief, unremarkable encounter outside a grocery store near downtown. She drops her groceries. He helps pick them up. Both go on their way. It revealed that Amber receives help without deflecting or over-thanking — proportional, contained; and that Cole helps without needing to be asked, acknowledged, or remembered, which is simply how he's built.

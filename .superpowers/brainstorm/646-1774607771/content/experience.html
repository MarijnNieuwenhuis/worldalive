What's the core experience?

The world has characters, scenes, a timeline, locations. What should someone feel when they open the site?

A

The Chronicle

A timeline you scroll through — scenes unfold in order, you can jump to any moment. Like reading a living story. Time travel is the main feature.

B

The Town Map

A map of Billings with character pins. Pick a moment in time and see where everyone is. Click a character to read their day. Space is the main navigation.

C

The Characters

A cast page — portraits, profiles, relationship webs. Dive into who someone is, their history, their diary. People are the main navigation.

D

All three, combined

A dashboard with map + timeline + character panel. Pick a time, see the map update, click a character. Everything connected.
